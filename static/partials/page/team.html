Team

The Red9 team philosophy is based on transparency, accountability and communication, which are critical to teamwork and motivation. Given our small team size and that each team member is intimately involved in all aspects of Red9, we currently operate in a flexible and dynamic environment. However, the team members have specific responsibilities.

Mike has 16 years of investment experience, 13 years of experience investing in small technology companies with TCW. He is currently the Portfolio Manager for the TCW Small & Mid Cap Growth Team.
